Print this cassette sleeve, fold it following the lines and put in a cassette case.
Look at it while listening to the music.
Organen is dutch for organs as you could have guessed.


(1) Organen: A composition for homemade shamisen-like cigarbox stringed instrument, played with a slide, and homemade shakuhachi with rather unpolished mouthpiece. All entirely repitched. No it's not a bass guitar.


(2) Freyheid: Dual indepedent improvisation with homemade shamisen-like cigarbox stringed instrument, played with a slide, and voice. Based on a composition of Jürg Frey (Petit fragment de paysage (2009) version for violin and viola).
