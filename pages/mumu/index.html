Ryoko Akama - Dial 45-21-95

The Another Timbre record label has been a great discovery for me. This somewhat Wandelweiser-ish album with music from Ryoko Akama I, unsurprisingly, like a lot. There is something in the track titles that makes the whole record resonate as something grounded in humble everyday life.

DJ Olive - Listening to Fir

A great record by DJ Olive I somehow missed. I must have slept hundreds of times to his Sleep, Buoy and Triage albums, and discovering there is something left to discover is a very pleasant surprise.
